# Mocha Pro — Professional Planar Tracking and Rotoscoping for Visual Effects

## About Mocha Pro

Mocha Pro provides a complete professional planar tracking and rotoscoping environment for visual effects artists, compositors, and post-production specialists. Mocha Pro combines surface tracking, object removal, and masking tools. Mocha Pro supports PowerMesh warped tracking, Mega Plates stitching, and lens calibration. Mocha Pro integrates with Adobe After Effects, Nuke, Fusion, and Avid Media Composer. Mocha Pro enables artists to perform screen inserts, match moves, beauty retouching, and wire/rig removal with planar precision. Mocha Pro is trusted by VFX artists, post houses, broadcast designers, and feature film studios worldwide.

Planar tracking solving perspective transformations on flat surfaces

Mega Plates stitching multiple clips into extended panoramic plates
Object removal with clean plate generation and hole filling
Lens calibration correcting distortion for accurate camera solve
